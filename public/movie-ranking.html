The Best Animes According to Varun

My top 3 animes of all time.

Attack On Titan

The best written show and the best piece of fiction, and is my no 1.

Jobless Reincarnation

The best isekai story ever written and is so satisfying.
The fantasy element is so beautiful.

Classroom of The Elite

One of the best psychological mind games show.
Although the sourcew material the "light novel" is mindblowing the anime isn't half bad but is not as good as the source.
